# Data Collection Guide

1.  Download Indian traffic datasets from sources like Roboflow Universe ("Indian traffic", "helmet detection", "license plate India").
2.  Also consider KAGGLE datasets.
3.  Target classes: `motorcycle`, `person`, `car`, `truck`, `bus`, `helmet`, `license_plate`
4.  Ensure at least 500 images representing Sangli/Pune conditions (or whatever location).
